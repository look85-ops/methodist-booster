# Scene 002 — Construction (Конструкция)

## Концепт
Два Pukuru строят башню из круглых камней. Один подаёт снизу, второй принимает наверху, лёжа на животе. Башня неровная.

Two humanoid creatures building a tower of round stones in an empty space. One creature below holds a stone up with both hands, stretching on tiptoes. Second creature lies on its stomach on top of the half-built tower, reaching down with long thin arms to receive the stone. The tower is uneven and slightly tilted.
