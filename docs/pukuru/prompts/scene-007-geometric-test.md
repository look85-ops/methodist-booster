# Scene 007 — Geometric Test (Геометрический тест)

## Концепт
Два геометрических Pukuru передают друг другу светящуюся сферу. Собраны из простых форм.

## Prompt (Midjourney)

Two creatures made of simple geometric shapes standing in empty space. Each creature constructed from distinct flat colored blocks: round head, rectangular body, thin rectangular limbs with circular joints. One creature slightly taller (teal and warm beige), one slightly shorter (coral and pale cream). Between them a perfect circle floating. Both have two small black dot eyes. No facial expression. Clean Bauhaus-style vector, flat colors, no outlines, no shading, no gradients, nothing painterly. Transparent background. Textile print ready.
