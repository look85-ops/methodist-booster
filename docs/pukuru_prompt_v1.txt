Prompt #1 — "Жемчужина" (Pearl Handoff)
Сцена для генерации. Дата: первая проба.

---
Two humanoid creatures standing facing each other on a flat blank space. First creature: tall and stretched, with very long thin arms ending in delicate fingers, small head, simple curious eyes and a tiny open mouth. Second creature: round and compact like a mollusk on two short legs, simple focused eyes, reaching one arm up toward the first creature. Between them a glowing pale blue pearl floating in the air, just above the second creature's fingertips. Both figures are brightly colored: the tall one in vivid teal and pale orange, the round one in coral and warm yellow. Flat vector illustration style, clean shapes, solid color fills, no outlines, no shadows, no gradients, no depth. Simple naive art feel, like a page from a collector's field guide. No background, no ground line, no texture. High contrast, vibrant colors on transparent background. Suitable for textile print.
